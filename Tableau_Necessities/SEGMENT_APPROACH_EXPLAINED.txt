THE PROBLEM WITH OLD APPROACH:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Input:  500 streets, 1 average point each
        Avenue A: (40.72, -73.97)
        Avenue B: (40.71, -73.96)
        Street 1: (40.73, -73.95)
        ...

What Tableau did: Connected them all together
        Avenue A ───> Avenue B ───> Street 1 ───> ...
                (ONE GIANT CONNECTED LINE across entire city)

Result: 🔴 WRONG - Can't tell streets apart, all connected in mess


THE SOLUTION WITH NEW APPROACH:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Input: 3,626 streets × avg 2-3 segments each = 7,840 total segments

Example - 1 AVENUE (showing multiple segments):
  Segment 1: (40.741, -73.976) Congestion=0.03 Incidents=2 Seq=1
  Segment 2: (40.648, -74.021) Congestion=0.03 Incidents=3 Seq=2
  Segment 3: (40.726, -73.987) Congestion=0.03 Incidents=3 Seq=3
  ...
  Segment 19: (40.786, -73.943) Congestion=0.04 Incidents=4 Seq=19

What Tableau does with Sequence + PATH:
  1. Takes all 19 segments with Street="1 AVENUE"
  2. Sorts by Sequence (1, 2, 3... 19)
  3. Draws ONE continuous line through all 19 points
  4. Colors each point by Congestion (blue/yellow/red)
  5. Sizes each point by Incidents (thickness)

Result: 🟢 CORRECT - 1 Avenue shows as ONE traceable line with congestion
        variations visible along its length!

TROUBLESHOOTING:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: It still looks like a connected mess?
A: Make sure Sequence is on PATH shelf
   Check that Street is on DETAIL shelf
   RIGHT-CLICK Sequence → Make sure it's "Continuous" (not Discrete)

Q: Lines look broken/disconnected?
A: Sequence field must be on PATH (not on columns/rows)
   Sequence should be numeric, in order

Q: How do I know it's working?
A: Each street should look like ONE connected line
   Different streets should NOT connect to each other
   Lines should follow actual street locations on the map
   Colors should show congestion variations WITHIN the street

Q: Can I see street names on the map?
A: Add Street to TOOLTIP:
   Drag Street → TOOLTIP
   Now hover over any segment to see street name + congestion data
